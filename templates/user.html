About

My name is William Casarin. I've started Satsbacker as a response to Pateron's abusive treatment of users that don't fit their perferred political mold. Satsbacker is open source alternative built on the Bitcoin Lightning network.

When running Satsbacker, the users and backers are in control. No service provider or payment processor can shut you down. As long as you have server with a connection to the internet, you can get paid by people who support your work.
